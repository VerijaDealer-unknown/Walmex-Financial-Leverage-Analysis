## Project Objective
Financial  analysis of Walmart Mexico and Centroamérica (Walmex), focusing in the relationship between profitability and debt load.

---

##Do companies with more debt tend to have lower or higher profits?
Higher debt directly relate to higher ROE for investors utilizing liabilities

## Key Insights & Financial Takeaways
* **Leverage Velocity:** The interactive *Correlation Chart* highlights how structural increases in the Debt-to-Equity ratio historically catalyzed ROE up to peak thresholds, proving the strategic use of financial leverage.
* **Risk Cushion:** Total liabilities remain strongly optimized against total shareholder equity, highlighting resilient debt-servicing safety margins even under margin compression.
